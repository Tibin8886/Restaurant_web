About Us

Relaxed elegance with a passion for flavour.

Olive & Ember is inspired by Mediterranean warmth and modern city dining. Our restaurant combines natural textures, soft lighting, and open-kitchen energy to create a welcoming atmosphere for dinners, celebrations, and casual gatherings.

Every dish is prepared with carefully selected ingredients, balanced flavours, and a focus on quality. Whether you are joining us for a quick lunch or a long evening meal, our goal is to make every visit memorable.
